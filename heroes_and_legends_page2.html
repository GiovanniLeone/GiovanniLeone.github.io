Stephen Hawking

Stephen Hawking has defied all emotional, physical and academic boundaries. He suffered from a disease called amyotrophic lateral sclerosis, a disease that paralyized him. He defied the doctor's prediction of a two year life expectancy in 1963. Despite Hawking's disadvantages, he still dedicated his life's work to academia and pubilshed A Brief History Of Time. His book that discusses his theories of cosmology.

Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs, the co-founder of Apple, was more of a flawed hero. He was considered very difficult to work with because of his intense desire for perfection. Nevertheless, he was still admired and loved by people who believed that being a part of Apple was special. Steve Jobs was a hero to the people who transformed their lives with Apple's products and by the people who worked for him. When Steve Jobs passed away, it was like the passing of a popular political leader to his customers.
